FARGO3D-DustRad Documentation

DustRad is a module for FARGO3D that adds fast treatments for dust evolution and radiative transfer to 2D hydrodynamic simulations of protoplanetary disks.

The dust evolution is based on the two population dust method fromTil Birnstiel's two-pop-py

The radiative transfer is adapted froma moment method developed by Hannah Jang-Condell
